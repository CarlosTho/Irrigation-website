Sprinkler Inspections and Repairs - Maintenance

Sprinkler Inspections and service visits should be performed at least once a year.

Our Process

Vergel Irrigation ensures that your lawn sprinkler system keeps your yard, flowers, and shrubs flourishing!

Seasonal inspections visits will make sure that your irrigation system is operating the way it should be. Here at Vergel Irrigation, there are NO CONTRACTS and NO PREPAYMENTS! You simply pay as you go with our lawn sprinkler inspections/repairs.

Looking for a maintenance plan or experiencing issues with your sprinkler system? You're in the right place! Just click here to send us a message and we'll be happy to assist you.

When we perform our lawn sprinklers inspections, this is what we do :

  • Adjust zones and heads/rotors for proper function, coverage and efficiency

  • Adjust sprinkler heads for minimal water waste

  • Clean sand or debris from any plugged sprinkler nozzles

  • Test all zones for sticking or leaking pressure seals on heads.

  • Check each zone for the correct performance pressure

  • Test irrigation valves for proper function including opening and closing

  • Inspect main lines, lateral lines and valve boxes for leaks

  • Test irrigation controller for proper cycling of irrigation zones

  • Check rain sensor connection

  • Recommend an irrigation schedule for you
