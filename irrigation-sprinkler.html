The Most Common Lawn Sprinkler Repairs

  1. Broken lawn sprinkler heads, PVC pipe leaks, valves or control timers
  2. Broken rain sensors, backflow preventers, or shutoff valves
  3. Removing/Capping Off sprinkler heads that you no longer need
  4. Overspray - if your sprinklers are watering beyond your lawn, then you are experiencing overspray. Usually, this is the result of improperly positioned sprinkler heads.
